Air Cargo Service

Door to Door

Your single source of Air freight services for carrying consignments to various destinations by using Air Transport. With direct access to prime capacities, your air cargo not only gets to the right place at the right time, it also gets specialized handling treatment till the time it reaches its final destination.

This service is very useful for time sensitive, urgent, high value, and critical shipments.

Hand carry Delivery

Under this service Satellite is able to deliver time sensitive, and urgent consignments to end users in record time by using multi modal system comprising of Air/Rail and surface mode and the consignment is accompanied by Satellite personnel.

Same day Delivery (NFO)

A unique service offering whereby clients have the option to move their shipment and get it delivered to their customer the same day by using the Next Flight Out Service.

Door to Airport

It is a service in which Satellite picks up the shipment(s) from the sender's office premises/ warehouse and book’s it to the destination airport. The consignee clears the load and picks up the consignment from the airport on the same day or next day.

This service is ideal for urgent, critical deliveries to satellite cities like Jammu, Calicut, Bhubaneswar, Ranchi, Mangalore, Raipur, Amritsar, Rajkot,Vijaywada, Visakhapatnam and to better the transit schedule followed by cargo/courier companies of 48hrs.

Under this service your consignment is booked directly with the Airline of customers’ choice, and selection of flight number.

Airport to Door

Under this service current and potential clients can take the advantage of booking consignments at the last moment at our Airport Hub, situated near the origin airport. The consignments need to be booked 3 hours prior to flight departure. The consignment gets cleared at destination airport by our staff member and immediately delivered to the consignee at their respective destination in minimum transit time.
